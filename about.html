About the ANTI-PC Project

ANTI-PC is a philosophy disguised as hardware. It is not a product. It is an engineered declaration of independence from corporate design dogma.

  • Fanless. Passive cooling only.
  • No RGB. No brand stickers. No nonsense.
  • Fully modular. Every part can be replaced or upgraded by hand.
  • Built with real components. Not a concept. Not a dream.

This is Version 0.1. Future versions may emerge. Or not. That's up to the network.
